Hawaii Dinosaur Volleyball Tournament

Play Till Extinct!

The most prehistoric game on the planets most beautiful courts

We are the fusion of ancient energy and elite beach volleyball action. Expect high-level play, unforgettable tournament experiences, and unparalleled camaraderie on the Hawaiian sand. Join us to challenge yourself under the Pacific sun.

A story 32 years in the making.

The start of an "old school" tradition.

In 1994, founders Marin Artukovich and David Chaikin created the Hawaii Dinosaur Volleyball tournament to give older players a platform for high-level "old school" beach volleyball.

Decades later, this cherished tradition continues to draw players for fierce competition and family fun at its beloved home on Kalapaki Beach, Kauai.

Spike like a master!

Competition is open to everyone, from our youngest Keiki to the toughest "Dino" Masters! Beyond the legendary high-age combined divisions, we invite family teams-like the popular Father/Son and Mother/Daughter brackets-to compete in a spirited environment that embraces all generations of beach volleyball players.
